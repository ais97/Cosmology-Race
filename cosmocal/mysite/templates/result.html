This graph shows who's leading the race.... and when someone overtook the others. Put in different parameter values and get the results you want!!! Remember, negative values are not allowed for most parameters and will throw errors.

Things to keep in mind:

  • You can enter all values depending on what kind of universe you envision.
  • Hubble parameter stands for the expansion of the universe.
  • Matter, Radiation and Dark energy are the known components of the Universe. Curvature is a property of the Universe. Altogether, these parameters must add up to 1. If the entered values don't follow this, then the curvature parameter is changed by the calculator.
  • Redshift is how far back from today and now you want to see. The present time has a redshift of 0. The Big Bang is at redshift ∞. Same difference in redshift doesn't give same difference in time, mind you!
  • The thing is, we can't really observe anything beyond a redshift of ~1100. So if you enter a redshift >1100, proceed at your own risk. The developers of this calculator are not responsible for any wrong calculations... you have been warned!!!
  • You can put random values and make your unique universe or you can read up and put the exact values observed. More fun in creating your own universe I think!!!
  • The default values entered for the parameters are more or less the observed values in our current universe. Redshift is given as an example.
  • The results will have a graph that explains the components of your universe.
  • This calculator is not perfect.😉 😉
